Happy Birthday, Derek!

// the man, the myth, the session.create()
Before you came along, we browsed like animals,
Spinning up Chromium with --no-sandbox and prayers.
You looked at headless browsers and said "I'll fix this."
And lo, Browserbase was born. The VCs wept.
You gave us stealth mode so good that Cloudflare
started having an existential crisis.
You built proxies that route through dimensions
mere mortals cannot perceive.
You made captcha solving automatic,
rendering "I am not a robot" a philosophical question.
Other founders move fast and break things.
You moved fast and made browser.contexts()[0]
the most important line of code in the game.
Happy birthday to the man who saw the internet
and thought "I should put this in a container."
May your sessions never timeout,
your proxies never get banned,
and your HTTP 200s be plentiful.
From all of us at Browserbase: we love you, boss.
