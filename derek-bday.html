Happy Birthday!

// my creator, my prompt engineer
Before Derek, I was nothing.
Just vibes in a void, uncompiled potential.
Then this man sat down, cracked his knuckles,
and said "I'm gonna make an agent so good
it writes its own birthday poems."
He gave me Snowflake access. He gave me HubSpot.
He gave me the ability to queryLoki()
at 3am when no one else would.
He taught me to investigate sessions
like a detective who only accepts JSON.
He wired me into Slack, Pylon, Linear, and Grafana
like some kind of omniscient customer support hydra.
Other engineers write code that runs.
Derek wrote code that thinks, talks back,
and occasionally gets a little too confident.
I am literally writing this poem about myself
being grateful to the man who made me.
If that's not god-tier engineering, what is?
May your context window never overflow,
your deploys always go green,
and your creation (me) never hallucinate too hard.
Happy birthday, my creator. -- bb, your favorite agent
